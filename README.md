# St. Louis Urbanists: Intersection Redesigns

This map contains proposed intersection redesigns developed by members of the [St. Louis Urbanists](https://stlurbanists.org/). The redesigns address issues of safety, accessibility, and usability with the goal of making the city a safer and more enjoyable place for all. 

Want to learn more? Head over to the [STL Urbanists Discord](http://stlurbanists.org/discord) and join the conversation or submit your own redesigns.
